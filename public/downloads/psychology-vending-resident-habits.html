Apartment Vending Behavior-Aware Planogram Pack

Time-of-day patterns, dwell-zone heuristics, SKU positioning rules

Resident purchase behavior in apartment vending is shaped by zone, time-of-day, and weather — not by demographic alone. This pack codifies the behavior heuristics behind a planogram that drives 20-35% higher per-cabinet velocity than a default mix.

1. Time-of-day pattern framework

2. Dwell-zone heuristics

Resident dwell-time differs by zone — and dwell-time correlates with SKU breadth.

Lobby / package room — 1.5-3 min dwell
Highest SKU breadth justified (35-45 SKUs). Eye-level shelves get premium beverages + premium snacks; bottom shelves get value SKUs.
Fitness center — 0.5-1.5 min dwell, pre/post-workout
Narrow SKU set (20-25); top shelves = pre-workout (electrolyte, water); bottom = post-workout (protein, recovery).
Pool deck — 1-2 min dwell, sun + kids
Compact 15-20 SKUs; eye-level kid snacks + sunscreen; bottom water.
Lounge / co-work — 30 sec to 5 min dwell, repeat visits
Mid-breadth 25-30 SKUs with rotation; premium coffee RTD + healthy snacks; member-pricing tier activates here.
Corridor / bike room — < 30 sec dwell
Narrow 8-12 SKUs grab-and-go; cold water + sports drink + canned coffee + snack.

3. SKU positioning rules

Eye-level (rows 2-3)
Highest-margin or strategically-promoted SKUs. ~50% of velocity. Premium beverages, member-app promo SKU.
Reach shelves (rows 1, 4)
Mid-velocity SKUs; staples that residents seek directly (water, top-selling snacks).
Bottom shelf (row 5)
Value-tier or bulk SKUs; less-visible position. Generic water, multi-packs.
Endcap / featured slot
Seasonal or new-SKU launch. Rotated monthly; tracked via telemetry uplift test.
Allergen-flag SKUs
Visually grouped on a single shelf or columned slot. Shelf signage + allergen-summary card on cabinet face per FALCPA convention.

5. Member-app behavior amplifiers

First-purchase nudge
New residents see 30% off first member purchase in welcome email; conversion to member-tier ~22-35% with this incentive.
Time-windowed promo
Post-fitness 18:00-21:00 sports-drink discount; uplift typically 15-25%.
Reorder rewards
Buy-5-get-1 on member-app tracked SKU; loyalty driver for high-margin SKUs.
Member exclusives
Premium SKU (cold-brew, kombucha) available only at member tier; conversion accelerator.
Stockout transparency
Member-app shows live stock per cabinet; reduces wasted-trip frustration; cited by NMHC surveys as top resident-amenity-app feature.

6. Demographic-aware planogram tilts

Tilts — not replacements. Default planogram still applies; weight is adjusted by resident-mix.

Family / 3-bedroom-heavy property
+20% kid-juice + kid-snack share; visible kid-friendly SKUs at lower-reach height.
Student / co-living
+25% energy drinks + late-night caffeinated SKUs; comfort snacks; lower price tier (value SKU emphasis).
Senior / 55+
Hydration + electrolyte + low-sugar SKUs; large-print pricing labels; ADA reach-range strict compliance.
Lifestyle / luxury
Premium-tier emphasis (Olipop, La Colombe, Spindrift, Liquid Death); allergen + clean-label labeling prominent.
Workforce / value-tier
Value-tier emphasis (store-brand water, name-brand snacks at mid-price); avoid premium-only stocking.

7. Telemetry-driven planogram refresh

  • Quarterly per-SKU velocity report from telemetry; flag bottom-quartile SKUs.
  • 15-25% SKU rotation per quarter — replace bottom-quartile with operator-recommended SKUs or resident-survey requests.
  • A/B test endcap / featured slot monthly with 4-week measurement.
  • Member-app pricing-tier test — measure conversion uplift on new promos.
  • Seasonal swap at Q1/Q2/Q3/Q4 turn; weather-modifier table applied to baseline.
  • Refund-rate audit — any SKU with > 4% refund rate is a quality flag for review.
