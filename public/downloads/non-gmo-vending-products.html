2. Why the program runs on Non-GMO Project Verified + USDA Organic

Non-GMO Project (NGP)
Independent nonprofit; product-mark on > 60,000 SKUs as of 2024. Action-level threshold 0.9% matches EU regulation. The most visible Non-GMO label in US grocery + workplace catalogs.
USDA Organic
Federal program (NOP). Categories: 100% Organic, Organic (≥ 95%), Made with Organic (≥ 70%). Excludes GMO at any level.
USDA BE Disclosure
Mandatory disclosure since 2022. Not a wellness signal — products labeled 'Contains BE Food' are the opposite. Useful as program guardrail (avoid BE-labeled SKUs).
What the labels mean for purchasing
NGP Verified + USDA Organic are operationally equivalent for vending purposes. Some employers stack ('NGP + Organic') for marketing clarity.

6. Sourcing + supplier criteria

  • Operator catalog filterable by Non-GMO Project Verified + USDA Organic status
  • Quarterly certification refresh — SKU certification status checked at each restock; expired certifications flagged
  • Suppliers attest annually to chain-of-custody for Non-GMO Project SKUs
  • USDA Organic SKUs sourced from NOP-accredited certifier supply chain
  • Imports (EU Organic, JAS Organic, Demeter) attested with destination-country equivalence
  • BE-labeled SKUs (USDA mandatory disclosure) explicitly excluded from Non-GMO planogram

7. Measurement framework

Velocity by certification tier
Per-SKU vends/day grouped by NGP / Organic / Conventional. Trend monthly.
Certified share of total vends
Target: archetype % within 30% band (e.g., 50% archetype → 35-65% actual).
Stockout rate on certified SKUs
Top-3 stockout ≤ 5% of days.
Satisfaction (NPS)
Quarterly survey; target NPS ≥ 30 year-1, ≥ 50 year-3.
ESG / wellness reporting
Annual report — % SKUs certified, % vends certified, supplier mix, certification refresh log.

8. Subsidy + pricing

Price premium
Non-GMO + Organic SKUs typically 10-25% premium over conventional. Subsidize to parity or below for first 6 months to build velocity.
Subsidy options
Free-vend on certified SKUs; flat-rate $1 for any certified SKU; wellness-app reward credit.
Budget guideline
$10-30/employee/month for 50% archetype; $25-50 for 70-100%.

9. Operator selection checklist

  • Catalog of ≥ 40 Non-GMO Project Verified or USDA Organic SKUs across 8+ categories
  • Quarterly certification refresh workflow demonstrated
  • Telemetry per-SKU velocity reporting
  • Allergen + ingredient disclosure CSV per SKU
  • Subsidy-flexible billing
  • Wellness-app integration capability
  • References from 3+ comparable ESG-aligned hosts with ≥ 24 months tenure
