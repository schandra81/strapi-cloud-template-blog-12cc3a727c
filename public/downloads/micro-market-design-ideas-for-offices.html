2. Fixture and equipment palette

Glass-door coolers
AHRI-certified, ENERGY STAR-listed multi-deck merchandisers (True, Hussmann, Hill Phoenix or equivalent). LED interior, anti-condensate doors. Target ≤41°F per FDA Food Code 2022 §3-501.16.
Ambient gondola shelving
Modular steel, 4-5 shelves, 48-72 in run lengths. Adjustable to support 16-24 in deep planogram.
Self-checkout kiosk
365 Retail Markets MicroMarket, Avanti Markets, Three Square Market, or comparable. EMV + NFC + biometric/app options. PCI-DSS 4.0 compliant.
Trash + recycling station
Three-stream minimum (landfill / recycle / compost) where the building supports compost. ADA-compliant openings ≥15 in from finished floor.
Microwave + coffee station
Two 1000-1200 W microwaves per 200 employees; bean-to-cup coffee or pour-over depending on tier. GFCI-protected 20A circuits per NEC 210.8.

3. Lighting design

  1. Ambient lighting

    30-50 footcandles at task surface (IES RP-3 for retail back-of-house). LED 3500-4000K for accurate food appearance. Maintain Color Rendering Index (CRI) ≥ 80.

  2. Cooler internal lighting

    LED strip integral to the cooler — replace any halogen with LED for energy + heat-load reasons.

  3. Accent lighting on healthy-share

    Spot LED on the healthy-share endcap. Thorndike et al. (Harvard 2014; 2020) found that visual prominence + light cueing lifts healthy-share velocity 10-30%.

  4. Energy compliance

    ASHRAE 90.1-2022 lighting power density (LPD) for refreshment areas: ≤ 1.0 W/sq ft. Verify against local energy code (Title 24, IECC 2021).

4. Theft and shrink mitigation

Industry-reported shrink in self-checkout micro-markets typically lands at 2-5% of revenue depending on workforce mix, visibility, and software controls. Design decisions reduce shrink before operations does.

  • Camera coverage of every shelving run + kiosk (1080p minimum, 30-day retention) — coordinate with workplace privacy policy + state two-party-consent rules where applicable
  • Kiosk facing the room entry so the cashier-less checkout is the visible reference point
  • Workplace badge or fob integration to the kiosk account (reduces card-skimming and supports employer subsidy)
  • Random audit cycle by the operator (every 60-90 days) reconciles physical inventory to POS telemetry
  • AI-vision shrink alerts on the kiosk software where available (365 Pico, Avanti Markets Insights)
  • Signage on the wall: 'this is an honor market; thank you for scanning every item'

5. Healthy-share integration in the layout

  1. Fresh-food cooler near entry

    Make the grab-and-go cooler the first surface members see. Eye-level fresh produce + protein-forward meals lifts healthy purchases without restricting choice.

  2. Healthy-share endcap

    Reserve one gondola endcap for ≤200 cal, ≤6g added sugar, ≤480mg sodium SKUs. Green-leaf shelf strip. Refresh quarterly with the RDN-reviewed planogram.

  3. Water + unsweetened beverages adjacent to coffee

    Adjacent placement lifts beverage healthy-share. Choice architecture, not restriction.

  4. Calorie + allergen visibility

    FDA 21 CFR 101.11 menu-labeling if chain coverage applies; FALCPA + FASTER Act allergen labels on every package; allergen filter in the kiosk.
