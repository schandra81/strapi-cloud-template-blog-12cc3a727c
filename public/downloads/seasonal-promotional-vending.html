2. Five proven promotional mechanics

  1. Free-vend week or day

    Host covers entire transaction cost for a week or day. Highest engagement mechanic; 80-95% activation among headcount. Cost: $4-12 per employee per day. Use sparingly (1-2× per quarter).

  2. Buy-one-get-one + bundle promos

    Operator-funded or co-funded. Bundles healthier + indulgent ('apple + chocolate bar = $1.50'). Cost-neutral; lifts plant-based + Fit Pick velocity 20-40%.

  3. Loyalty stamp / punch card via member app

    10th drink free, 5th plant-based bar free. Cantaloupe Seed, 365 Pay, USConnect support natively. Drives repeat behavior over 3-6 month windows.

  4. ERG + DEI sponsored SKUs

    ERG selects 4-6 SKUs aligned with heritage month; subsidized or featured-position planogram. Engages ERG members + cross-pollinates education.

  5. Wellness-app-linked challenges

    Steps-to-snack, hydration-to-water, mindfulness-to-coffee credits via Virgin Pulse / Wellable / Limeade. Adds 10-25% incremental engagement at low marginal cost.

3. ERG / DEI integration playbook

Each ERG curates a tentpole promotion annually. Workplace-experience + ERG council co-design the SKU + signage + activation.

Discovery
ERG submits 6-8 SKU candidates 90 days ahead. Operator validates availability + nutrition labeling.
Sourcing
Prefer brands owned by the ERG-aligned community (Black-owned, women-owned, AAPI, LGBTQ+, Hispanic, Native, Disability-owned). Certifications: NMSDC, WBENC, NGLCC, NaVOBA.
Curation + signage
ERG provides backstory + brand-founder story for in-machine signage or member-app push notification.
Subsidy
ERG budget or central-DEI fund subsidizes featured SKUs to parity with conventional during the month.
Activation
ERG + L&D host a 'meet the founder' webinar, tasting event, or hallway pop-up. Operator coordinates SKU delivery.
Measurement
Per-SKU velocity over the month + ERG-member survey + cross-pollination tracking (non-ERG-member purchases of featured SKUs).

Member opt-in to ERG affiliation triggers tailored push (e.g., Black ERG gets Black History Month promo announcement first).

6. Hydration + wellness-event tie-ins

  • Annual wellness fair: free-vend day + biometric screening table + nutrition educator on-site
  • Hydration challenge (June or July): free sparkling water + branded reusable bottle distribution
  • Mindfulness Month (October or May): adaptogenic beverages (Recess, Kin Euphorics, Cure) featured + EAP collaboration
  • Steps challenge: wellness-app steps unlock free or discounted protein snack
  • Office sports league + after-work events: hydration + post-workout protein available 24/7
  • EAP awareness: stress-supportive beverages + signage with EAP contact info

7. Measurement framework

Promotion participation rate
Unique employees engaging / total headcount. Target: ≥ 40% on free-vend day, ≥ 20% on subsidized promo, ≥ 10% on loyalty challenge.
Same-period vends uplift
Promo period vends vs. same period prior year (or 4-week trailing average). Target: 15-40% uplift on featured SKUs.
Repeat-purchase rate
% of promo-period purchasers who repeat the SKU within 30 days of promo end. Target: 25-40% for trial-stage SKUs.
ERG + DEI engagement score
ERG member survey: did the program feel authentic + supportive? Target: ≥ 80% positive.
Cross-pollination
% of non-ERG-member purchases of ERG-featured SKUs. Target: ≥ 60% (program reaches beyond ERG).
Cost-per-engagement
Total promo cost / unique-employees-engaged. Target: ≤ $5-15 depending on promotion type.

8. Annual planning + governance

  • Q4 prior year: Workplace-experience + ERG council + benefits team agree annual calendar
  • Q1 + Q2 + Q3 + Q4: Quarterly planning session with operator (60 days ahead) to confirm SKUs + signage + subsidy
  • Monthly: Operator confirms previous month metrics; flag underperformers for course-correction
  • QBR: Full review of promotion mix + cost + uplift + engagement; recommend year-2 calendar adjustments
  • Annual: Member-app push opt-in audit + privacy policy refresh
  • Brand partnership pipeline: 3-5 prospective brands in discovery at any time

9. Operator selection checklist

  • Member-app + loyalty + push-notification platform (Cantaloupe, 365, USConnect, Hangar 12)
  • Wellness-app integration capability (Virgin Pulse, Wellable, Limeade)
  • Catalog flexibility for ERG / DEI / brand-partnership SKU additions on 30-day cycle
  • Subsidy-flexible billing (line-item per SKU per promo)
  • References from 3+ comparable-headcount hosts running active promotional calendars ≥ 18 months
  • Brand-partnership relationships demonstrated (3+ active national or regional brand seeding programs)
