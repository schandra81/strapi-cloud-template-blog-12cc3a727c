Apartment Vending Program Design Template

Property-type sizing, zone planning, operator RFP framework

Apartment vending sizing depends on property type, unit count, zone-by-zone resident behavior, and amenity-grade target. This template gives owners and property managers a defensible operator RFP grounded in NMHC and NAA benchmarks.

2. Zone planning framework

Resident behavior varies by amenity zone. Planogram + format choices follow zone, not building.

Lobby / mail / package room
Highest dwell — 1.5-3 min per visit. Best for beverage cooler, premium snack, micro-market. Convenience SKUs (umbrellas, batteries) viable.
Fitness center
Pre + post-workout traffic. Sports drinks, water, protein bars, recovery snacks. Avoid sugary sodas in branded fitness spaces.
Pool deck
Seasonal Q2-Q3 peak. Bottled water, sports drinks, kid snacks, sunblock, ice cream (if cabinet available). Hardened outdoor cabinet required.
Resident lounge / co-work
All-day dwell. Coffee + tea + snack + beverage. Member-app pricing tier recommended.
Dog wash / pet area
Niche but high-engagement. Treat dispenser, waste-bag dispenser, paw-wipe — small-format specialty machine.
Bike room / parking corridor
Quick-grab essentials. Cold beverage + snack combo.

4. Operator RFP template

Issue to 3-5 operators. Score using the rubric in Section 5.

  1. Background

    Operator company, years in multifamily, references with comparable unit-count + tier.

  2. Equipment proposal

    Cabinet make/model per zone, ENERGY STAR certification, finish + branding options, telemetry platform.

  3. Service spec

    Restock cadence, telemetry-driven dispatch, four-tier SLA, refund SLA, monthly scorecard format.

  4. Planogram

    Per-zone SKU mix with healthy-vending share, allergen labeling, member-app pricing tier, seasonal cadence.

  5. Commission framework

    Tiered commission per gross-revenue range; settlement cadence; audit rights.

  6. Closed-loop integration

    Member-app or building-app SDK support (badge / QR / app pay); credit-funding model.

  7. Insurance + compliance

    GL $2M, auto, product liability; ADA attestation; state retail food permit if fresh.

  8. Term + exit

    Term length, removal cost responsibility, equipment-return condition.

7. Resident communication plan

  • Resident-portal announcement at install — location, SKUs, payment methods, refund flow.
  • Resident-app deep link to operator app (member-pricing tier if applicable).
  • Lobby signage with QR-to-refund + service ticket.
  • Onboarding email for new leases — amenity-stack section includes vending FAQs.
  • Quarterly resident-feedback survey — top SKU requests, satisfaction score.
  • Property-manager direct-line to operator dispatch for resident escalations.
