2. Fob and access-control integration

  1. Access-control stack

    Tie the market door to the property's existing system — Brivo, Openpath/Avigilon Alta, LiftMaster myQ Community, ButterflyMX, Latch, Salto, Schlage Engage. Resident-only fob/mobile credential during designated hours.

  2. Door hardware

    Electric strike or magnetic lock with NFPA 101 free-egress hardware on the exit side. Coordinate with AHJ — magnetic locks require fire-alarm release and emergency push-to-exit.

  3. Camera + audit log

    Camera covers entry + kiosk; access log retained 60-90 days. Property management receives incident-mode replay link.

  4. Kiosk account

    Resident creates account in 365 Retail Markets / Avanti / Three Square via property-emailed welcome link. Payment by Apple/Google Pay, card, or property-portal billing (where supported).

3. Late-night planogram (the peak)

Multifamily transaction telemetry routinely shows 30-50% of revenue between 9pm and 3am. The planogram weights for late-night consumption while keeping daytime fresh-food coverage.

Snacks + indulgent
Chips, pretzels, popcorn, cookies, candy bars, ice cream novelty — 30-40% of facings. Late-night purchase pattern.
Beverages
Water, sodas, energy drinks, cold coffee, sports drinks — 25-30% of facings. Energy + cold coffee surge after 10pm.
Quick meals
Mac & cheese cups, ramen, microwave entrées, frozen pizza singles — 10-15%. Microwave on-site is critical for redemption.
Fresh food (cooler)
Salads, sandwiches, wraps, fruit, yogurt — 15-20%. Daytime + resident-event support; manage 7-day discard window per FDA Food Code 2022 §3-501.17.
Essentials
OTC pain relief, condoms, toothbrush kits, single-use detergent pods, batteries, phone chargers — 5-10%. Highest margin and highest stickiness.
Pet treats
Single-serve dog treats — 2-3% of facings. Disproportionate resident-NPS lift.

5. Operational SLA for unmanned 24/7

  • Telemetry-driven restock 2-3x/week minimum; daily during onboarding and during resident-event weeks
  • Tier 1 (offline / payment down / cold-chain) <24 hr acknowledgement, <24 hr resolution
  • Tier 2 (single SKU stockout) 24-48 hr acknowledgement, 5-7 day resolution
  • Refund flow via kiosk app / SMS — no on-site staff required for resident credits
  • Quarterly business review with property GM: revenue, traffic, refund rate, resident-NPS pulse
  • Cooler temperature alert (>41°F for >30 min) auto-pages on-call tech (FDA Food Code 2022 §3-501.16)

6. Amenity-ROI measurement

  1. Resident NPS lift

    Annual or quarterly resident survey carries a 'micro-market' line item. NMHC / Kingsley benchmarks let property compare amenity score vs. peer set.

  2. Lease-up impact

    Track the rate at which prospects mention the market on tour. Properties report 1-3% higher tour-to-lease conversion when the market is featured.

  3. Renewal lift

    Carry the market into the renewal package as a retained amenity. Even a 0.5-percentage-point renewal lift on a 300-unit property pays for the program many times over.

  4. Concierge / staff offload

    Resident requests for 'do you have...' decline measurably once the market opens. Track on-property task volume.

  5. Resident-event tie-in

    Free-vend stipends during property events (move-in week, holiday parties) at $X/resident — operator bills the property; resident receives the experience.
