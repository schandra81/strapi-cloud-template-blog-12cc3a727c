Healthy Snack Vending Pack

Threshold framework, SKU catalog by category, planogram templates, and measurement framework

This pack consolidates the working framework that wellness directors, food-service managers, and benefits leaders use to operationalize healthy vending. It covers the Fit Pick / FDA / Smart Snacks threshold stack, a SKU catalog by category, three planogram archetypes (30/50/70/100%), and the velocity + satisfaction measurement model that keeps the program from quietly reverting.

5. Hydration + fresh-fruit integration

  • Bottle-fill station adjacent to vending (Elkay LZSTL8WSLK, Halsey Taylor) — cuts beverage SKU stockouts + supports healthier defaults
  • Free fresh-fruit basket (banana, apple, orange) at start of week; 3-5 lb basket per 100 employees
  • Refrigerated fresh-cut (apple slices, baby carrots, hummus cups) where the unit is glass-front refrigerated; restock 2-3×/week
  • Plant-based milk + alt options (Oatly, Califia, fairlife Core Power) refrigerated section
  • Caffeine alternatives: green tea, sparkling water with caffeine, kombucha for healthcare + university hosts

6. Allergen + dietary inclusivity

Allergen labeling
FALCPA-compliant allergen statement on every SKU. Top-9 disclosure (milk, egg, fish, shellfish, tree nut, peanut, wheat, soy, sesame).
Gluten-free
≥ 15% of healthier SKUs gluten-free (KIND Healthy Grains certain SKUs, RXBAR, Larabar, Chomps).
Vegan + plant-based
≥ 20% vegan SKUs (Sun-Maid, Skinny Pop, Larabar, GoMacro, Bare Apple Chips).
Nut-free section
Required in K-5 schools; recommended in healthcare. Concentrated in dedicated row with clear signage.
Religious + cultural
Kosher (OU + OK + Star-K) and Halal options where employee population requires.

7. Measurement + course-correction framework

Healthy vending fails when measurement stops. Operator supplies this dashboard monthly; wellness committee reviews quarterly.

Velocity by SKU
Per-SKU vends/day. Healthier SKUs should reach 70-90% of conventional velocity within 6 months.
Healthier share of total vends
Trend monthly. Target: archetype % within 30% (i.e., 50% archetype = 35-65% actual share of vends).
Stockout incidents
Top-3 healthier SKUs stockout ≤ 5% of days. Stockouts kill program credibility faster than any other failure.
NPS / satisfaction
Quarterly 5-point smile-rating or NPS survey. Target NPS ≥ 30 in year 1, ≥ 50 by year 3.
Wellness biometric link
Optional: correlate vending vs. annual biometric screening trends (BMI, A1C). Requires HR + benefits collaboration.
Quarterly course-correction
Bottom-10% velocity healthier SKUs swap out for new SKUs; top performers held for the year.

8. Subsidy + price strategy

Subsidy options
Free-vend on healthier SKUs; flat-rate ($1 for any healthier SKU); price-match (healthier same price as conventional); wellness-app reward credit.
Price elasticity
Healthier SKUs at 10-20% premium over conventional usually see 30-50% velocity reduction. Subsidize to parity or below for first 6 months.
Cost-per-employee
$8-25/employee/month typical subsidy budget for 50% Fit Pick archetype; $20-45 for 70-100% archetype.
Benefits + ROI linkage
Healthcare claims data + biometric screening data show 8-15% lower diabetes + CVD claims in mature healthier-vending programs (ACSM + AHA studies; LetUsVending blog references).

9. Operator selection checklist

  • Catalog of ≥ 60 Fit Pick Select-aligned SKUs across 8+ categories
  • Telemetry-verified per-SKU velocity reporting
  • Quarterly planogram refresh based on velocity + employee feedback
  • Allergen + dietary inclusivity attestation per SKU
  • Nutrition + ingredient data file refreshed quarterly
  • Subsidy-flexible billing (line-item per SKU, not single flat rate)
  • Wellness-app integration capability (Virgin Pulse, Wellable, Limeade)
  • References from comparable-headcount hosts with ≥ 24 months tenure
