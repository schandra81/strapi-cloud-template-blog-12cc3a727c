Office Coffee Service Selection Framework

RFP + capability matrix + service model + bean tier + maintenance SLA

This framework gives facilities, HR, and procurement a structured way to evaluate office coffee service (OCS) providers. References to NSF/ANSI 4 commercial refrigeration, NSF/ANSI 18 manual dispensing equipment, UL 197 commercial electric cooking appliances, ENERGY STAR for commercial brewers, ADA Section 308 for accessible operable parts, and PCI-DSS 4.0 where the program includes account or payment-linked OCS.

3. Capability rubric + maintenance SLA

Reference accounts (≥ 3 comparable offices ≥ 12 months in service)
5 = ≥ 5 in matching size band; 3 = ≥ 3; 1 = none in size band.
Equipment specification + ENERGY STAR / NSF compliance
Operator should provide model list with NSF/ANSI 4 + 18 certification + ENERGY STAR where commercially available.
Tier-1 service SLA (machine offline, brew failure)
5 = ≤ 24 hr ack + resolution; 3 = ≤ 48 hr; 1 = no contractual SLA.
Tier-2 service SLA (descaling, filter, calibration)
5 = scheduled per cadence + on-demand within 5 days; 3 = scheduled only; 1 = on-call.
Water filtration program
5 = under-counter cartridge (3M / Everpure / Pentair) with documented replacement; 3 = scheduled; 1 = no filtration.
Bean origin + certification share
5 = ≥ 50% fair-trade / rainforest-alliance / organic; 3 = ≥ 30%; 1 = no certification commitment.
Sustainability + cup waste
5 = BPI-certified compostable cup option + recycling capture; 3 = recyclable cup; 1 = standard non-recyclable.
Account management + QBR cadence
5 = quarterly QBR + monthly stand-up; 3 = quarterly QBR; 1 = annual review only.
Pricing transparency
5 = published per-cup / per-month + supply pass-through detail; 3 = monthly statement; 1 = bundled invoice without detail.
Insurance + liability
$2M GL + $1M umbrella + additional-insured endorsement; food product liability ≥ $1M.
