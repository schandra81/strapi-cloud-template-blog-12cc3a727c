Resident Lounge Snack Vending Design Template

Cabinet selection, planogram per lounge type, operator RFP

Resident lounge vending is a different animal from corridor vending — long dwell-time, repeat visits, member-app pricing tier, and a planogram that flexes with co-work vs social-lounge use. This template covers cabinet selection, planogram per lounge type, and the operator RFP framework.

1. Lounge-type taxonomy

2. Cabinet selection per lounge type

Co-work / business center
Open-shelf micro-market (Avanti, 365 Retail Markets) or AI-vision smart cooler (AWM Smart Shelf); 60-100 SKU capacity; self-checkout kiosk; ENERGY STAR commercial refrigerator base.
Social lounge / clubroom
Glass-front beverage cooler (Royal Vendors RVCC ENERGY STAR V3.0) + snack combo (USI 3014A); 30 + 45 SKUs; high-traffic capacity.
Game room / media lounge
Combo snack/beverage cabinet with kid-friendly SKU mix; lower-reach-height visibility for younger residents; cashless + member-app tap.
Hybrid
Smart cooler (Nayax Onyx RFID, AWM AI vision) + glass-front beverage; member-app SDK for pricing tier; broadest SKU breadth.

4. Member-app pricing tier

Member tier (resident)
Base × 0.85-0.95. Auth via member-app SDK, RFID badge, or building-app QR.
Guest tier (visitor)
Base × 1.0. Cashless tap or cash; subject to event-rental override pricing.
Event-mode pricing
Lounge in private-event mode (host-paid) — resident sees $0; host invoiced. Toggle via property-manager admin in operator dashboard.
Time-windowed promo
Co-work hours (07:00-19:00) discount on coffee + healthy snacks; uplift typically 15-25%.

5. Operator RFP requirements

  1. Background

    Multifamily portfolio depth; references from properties with comparable lounge programming.

  2. Equipment

    Cabinet make/model per lounge type; ENERGY STAR certification; ADA reach-range attestation; brand-finish capability.

  3. Service spec

    Restock cadence per lounge type; telemetry; four-tier SLA; refund SLA.

  4. Planogram

    Per-lounge-type planogram document; allergen labeling; healthy-vending share; seasonal cadence.

  5. Member-app integration

    Payment platform + building-app SDK compatibility; UAT plan; auth model; refund flow.

  6. Reporting

    Monthly scorecard with per-cabinet uptime, top SKU, refund rate, member-mix; BI export channel.

  7. Commission framework

    Tiered commission per gross-revenue range; event-mode billing model; settlement cadence.

  8. Insurance + compliance

    GL $2M, product + auto, ADA, food permit if fresh.

7. Lounge-specific operational considerations

  • Co-work — quiet-restock window (early-morning or after-hours) to avoid disrupting calls.
  • Social lounge — restock before peak event nights (Thursday-Sunday); fresh SKU restock + waste disposal.
  • Game room — kid-safe SKU positioning; clear allergen labeling; no choke-hazard items.
  • Hybrid — sensor-light cabinets that don't pulse during quiet co-work hours; ambient-aware UI.
  • Event-mode toggle accessible to property manager (no operator-side ticket required for simple toggle).
  • Trash + recycling within 25 ft of every cabinet.
  • Cellular signal verified > -95 dBm; Wi-Fi credentialed fallback.
