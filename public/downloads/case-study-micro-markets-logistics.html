Logistics Hub Micro-Market Playbook

Space design, planogram template, shrink management framework, SLA matrix, worker-communication kit

A micro-market replaces 4-8 vending machines with an open-shelf retail format where workers self-checkout via kiosk or app. The format unlocks 3-5x SKU breadth and 20-40% velocity uplift vs equivalent vending square footage — but introduces shrink, ADA, and PCI exposure your operator must be equipped to manage. This playbook codifies the operational framework.

1. Space design — when a micro-market fits

Not every break room is a micro-market candidate. Three baseline thresholds must be met.

Headcount per access window
≥ 75 workers passing through the space per shift, or ≥ 200 per day. Below this, four vending machines outperform on velocity-per-square-foot.
Footprint
180-400 ft² typical: 60-90 linear feet of shelving + 1-2 cold cases + 1-2 freezers + 1-2 self-checkout kiosks. Below 150 ft², a 'micro-pantry' (cooler + open snack tower) is the right format.
Security posture
Camera coverage + badge access + worker accountability culture. Hubs with badge-controlled entry to the break room run 1-3% shrink; uncontrolled access runs 5-15%.

3. Shrink management framework

Micro-markets run 1-5% shrink at well-managed hubs; 10-15% if controls are weak. The host and operator share responsibility.

  1. Camera coverage

    ≥ 2 cameras covering all shelves + kiosk station, 30-day retention minimum. Cameras owned and managed by the host (avoids operator privacy posture conflict).

  2. Kiosk + app account binding

    Worker checkout requires payroll-deduct enrollment or pre-funded account tied to badge or email. Anonymous cash transactions accepted but tracked separately.

  3. Inventory audit cadence

    Operator audits inventory weekly at restock; monthly variance report shared with host. Variance > 3% triggers root-cause review.

  4. Shrink-share contract

    Above a documented shrink threshold (typically 3%), host and operator share the excess at a contracted ratio (commonly 50/50). Below threshold, operator absorbs.

  5. High-shrink SKU rotation

    SKUs with persistent shrink > 5% pulled from open shelf to cold case (locked door) or removed. Premium meat snacks, energy drinks, and razor blades most commonly affected.

4. ADA + accessibility

Reach range — Section 308.2.1
Highest operable part of kiosk + cold-case door ≤ 48 inches above finished floor; lowest ≥ 15 inches. Top shelves ≤ 48 inches for reachable SKUs (lower shelves required for short-stature + wheelchair-user access).
Operating force — Section 309.4
Cold-case + freezer doors ≤ 5 lbf. Kiosk touchscreen + payment reader operable with one hand without tight grasping.
Aisle clearance — Section 403
Aisles ≥ 36 inches; passing space ≥ 60 inches at one point in each aisle.
Signage — Section 703
Pricing characters ≥ 5/8 inch; contrast ratio ≥ 70%; tactile characters at kiosk if host elects (not strictly required for self-service retail).
Kiosk software
VPAT provided by kiosk vendor. Screen-reader audio output, large-character mode, high-contrast mode.

5. PCI DSS + payment posture

  1. Kiosk PCI DSS attestation

    Kiosk vendor provides current PCI DSS attestation (Level 1 or 2). Card data tokenized at the reader; never stored on operator systems.

  2. Network segmentation

    Kiosk on dedicated VLAN; isolated from host operational and corporate networks.

  3. Payroll-deduct integration

    Where the host elects payroll deduction, the integration runs through the host's HRIS (Workday, UKG, ADP, Ceridian) via SFTP file or API. Operator never holds employee SSN or DOB.

  4. Refund flow

    Worker initiates refund via kiosk, app, or QR code. Cashless refunds processed within 24 hours; payroll-deduct refunds back out of next pay period.

7. Worker-communication kit

  • Multi-language welcome signage (English + Spanish minimum; additional by hub demographic)
  • Pictographic shelf-tag labels for low-literacy SKU recognition
  • Refund-flow card at kiosk (QR-to-app + phone fallback)
  • Payroll-deduct enrollment instructions (where host elects)
  • ADA + reach-range complaint channel
  • Shift-huddle launch deck for HR + EHS at go-live

8. Quarterly business review

  • Uptime report by kiosk + cold-case + freezer
  • Per-category velocity vs planogram-target ratio
  • Shrink variance report + root-cause review (if > 3%)
  • Refund volume + reason codes
  • ADA recertification status (annual)
  • PCI DSS attestation refresh status (annual)
  • Worker feedback summary
  • Action items + owner + due date
