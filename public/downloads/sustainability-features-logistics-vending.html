5. Quarterly ESG reporting template

Pull these eight metrics into a single dashboard. Each is operator-attestable and traces to a defined source.

  • Total kWh consumed (per-cabinet sub-meter where available; else manufacturer datasheet × cabinet count × hours)
  • Refrigerant GWP weighted across fleet (Σ (cabinet count × refrigerant GWP) / total cabinets)
  • Plant-based slot % (count + share of total)
  • Local + certified supplier slot % (≤ 250 mi or certified per #4 above)
  • Recyclable / compostable packaging slot %
  • Route miles per restock visit (telemetry-driven dispatch baseline)
  • Food waste % by units (expired SKUs pulled / total stocked)
  • Refurbished cabinet count + share of fleet at this site
