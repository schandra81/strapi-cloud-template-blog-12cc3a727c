24/7 Shift-Work Vending Operational Playbook

Restock cadence, service SLA, shift-aware planogram, and refund procedure

This playbook codifies the operating practices that keep a 24/7 vending program running at 98%+ uptime across three shifts. Each section maps to a measurable SLA your operator can attest to in writing.

1. Telemetry baseline

Required telemetry
Cellular-modem-enabled machines with sub-15-minute heartbeat. Cantaloupe, Nayax, 365 Retail Markets, AWM Smart Shelf, or comparable.
Metrics monitored
Online/offline status, last-restock timestamp, per-SKU velocity, cash/cashless mix, payment-failure rate, internal temperature for refrigerated units.
Alert thresholds
Offline > 30 minutes triggers operator alert. Temperature excursion outside 35-40°F (refrigerated) triggers immediate alert with 4-hour SLA.

3. Service SLA (operator-side)

  1. Tier 1 — revenue-critical / cold-chain / payment failure

    <24 hour acknowledgement, <24 hour resolution. Examples: machine offline, payment system down, temperature excursion.

  2. Tier 2 — stockout / planogram refresh

    24-48 hour acknowledgement, 5-7 day resolution. Examples: single SKU stockout, planogram update request.

  3. Tier 3 — cosmetic / signage

    5-day acknowledgement, 14-day resolution. Examples: signage replacement, cabinet cleaning, label refresh.

4. Shift-aware planogram

Shift-1 (day) and shift-3 (overnight) workers have different purchase patterns. Shift-1 weights breakfast + lunch; shift-3 weights caffeine + hot snacks + comfort foods. Telemetry tracks per-shift velocity so the planogram adjusts each quarterly review.

Shift-1 (6 AM – 2 PM)
Breakfast bars, fresh fruit, yogurt, coffee, water, balanced lunch combo.
Shift-2 (2 PM – 10 PM)
Snacks + sodas + energy drinks + lunch leftovers.
Shift-3 (10 PM – 6 AM)
Caffeinated beverages, hot drinks, comfort snacks, calorie-dense meal replacements.

5. Refund procedure

  1. Member-initiated refund

    Member triggers refund through operator app, on-machine touchpad, or SMS-to-refund number. Auto-credit within 24 hours for cashless transactions.

  2. Cash refund

    Member fills out refund slip; operator credits at next visit (max 7 days) or issues check via host HR/admin where applicable.

  3. Refund audit

    Quarterly refund report from operator to host. Refund rate > 2% of transactions triggers planogram + machine quality review.

6. Quarterly business review checklist

  • Uptime report (per-machine, per-month over the quarter)
  • Per-shift velocity report (shift-1 vs shift-2 vs shift-3)
  • Stockout incidents + resolution time
  • Refund volume + reason codes
  • Service ticket SLA adherence
  • Planogram refresh recommendations
  • Action items + owner + due date
This playbook is a working operational reference. Update the SLA + cadence sections to match your specific operator contract.
