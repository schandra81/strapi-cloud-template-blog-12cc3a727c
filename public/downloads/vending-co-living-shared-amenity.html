4. Member-app SDK integration checklist

  1. Authentication

    Member-app token (Common Living app, Outpost app, Tripalink app, Bungalow app) or NFC RFID member-badge integrated with PMS / building-app.

  2. Payment-platform compatibility

    Cantaloupe Engage / Nayax Onyx / 365 Pay SDK; confirm building-app vendor has SDK interop.

  3. Pricing tier

    Member tier (resident) — base × 0.85-0.95. Guest tier — base × 1.0. New-member welcome credit ($10-25).

  4. Credit funding source

    Member card-on-file (most common at co-living scale), payroll-deduction equivalent (rare), or host-stipend (small budgets only).

  5. Refund flow

    In-app refund button, SMS-to-refund, on-machine touchpad; auto-credit within 24 hours for cashless.

  6. Stock visibility

    Member-app shows live stock per cabinet — reduces wasted-trip frustration in shared amenity context.

  7. UAT

    2-cabinet pilot with 30-50 members over 2-3 weeks; decline-rate, double-charge, stock-update latency, refund-flow friction.

5. Brand finish framework

Co-living brands (Common, Outpost, Quarters, Tripalink, Bungalow) operate with strong visual identity. Cabinet finish should match brand standards, not generic operator wraps.

Vinyl wrap
3M IJ180Cv3 / Avery MPI 1105 cast vinyl with 3M 8520 / Avery DOL 1480 laminate; brand-palette CMYK + Pantone match; $400-$900 per cabinet.
Powder coat (front door)
TGIC polyester powder coat in brand color; furniture-grade; $600-$1,400 per cabinet refurb cycle.
Custom panel insert
Brand-logo + member-app QR + community-events deep-link printed on aluminum composite; sized to OEM cabinet door.
Illuminated marquee
LED edge-lit marquee with brand wordmark + community welcome message; time-of-day messaging (e.g., 'good morning' / 'after-hours snack').
Artwork deliverables
Vector EPS / AI / PDF at 1:1 scale, CMYK + Pantone, 0.25 in bleed; photography ≥ 300 dpi.

7. Reporting + governance

  • Monthly per-cabinet scorecard — uptime, top SKU, refund rate, member-mix, cashless mix.
  • Member-tier conversion report — % residents on member tier, avg member transactions/month.
  • Per-floor / per-zone velocity report — drives planogram refresh.
  • Refund-rate audit; > 2% triggers quality review.
  • Quarterly business review with brand-finish refresh check + member-survey results.
  • Annual portfolio review for multi-location co-living brands — standardized planogram refresh.
