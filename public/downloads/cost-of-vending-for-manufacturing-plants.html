3. Subsidy economics — three mechanics

$/shift credit
Typical $3–$8/shift loaded against badge. 250-FTE × 240 shifts × 50% participation × $5/shift = $150,000/year.
$/pay-period credit
Typical $20–$40 per bi-weekly period. 250-FTE × 26 periods × 50% participation × $25 = $81,250/year.
Category subsidy
Subsidy applied only to wellness or hydration categories. Drives wellness without paying for soda; typical reduction ~30–50% vs blanket subsidy.
Discount-at-PoS
Operator applies fixed % discount (15–25%); employer reimburses operator monthly. Simplest mechanic; least targeting.

5. Hidden + overlooked costs

Electrical install (one-time)
$400–$1,500 per dedicated 20A circuit if not already in place.
Network / cellular signal kit
$0–$800 per cabinet if facility cellular weak; usually included in operator quote.
ADA recertification (annual)
$200–$500/machine; sometimes line-item, sometimes bundled.
PCI-DSS reader lifecycle refresh
$0 in-contract typical; $350–$650/reader at end of 5–7 year lifecycle.
Workforce comms + signage
$400–$1,800 at launch; refresh at major planogram changes.
Payroll-deduct integration consulting
$0–$3,500 one-time if integrating with Workday/Kronos/UKG for first time.
Operator turnover transition
$2,000–$8,000 one-time if recompete or operator change mid-contract.
End-of-life equipment removal
$0 in full-service typical; verify in contract.

6. Operator selection checklist — cost transparency

  • Quote breaks down commission %, service fee, and subsidy admin transparently
  • All hidden / overlooked costs (above) explicitly listed in quote or marked 'included'
  • PCI-DSS v4.0 AoC on file; AoC term + cost included
  • Insurance certificates: $1M/$2M GL + product liability + cyber if cashless
  • Reference checks (3 minimum) from manufacturing sites of comparable size + complexity
  • Termination / transition cost capped in contract
  • Service credit remedy for SLA breach defined in dollars or % of fee
  • Audit rights — operator opens commission books to host on 30-day notice
  • Per-shift telemetry dashboard accessible to facility services + HR
