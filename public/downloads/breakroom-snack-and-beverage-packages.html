Office Snack and Beverage Package Toolkit

Survey template, archetype selector, planogram framework, ESG sourcing checklist, and quarterly review template

This toolkit helps workplace-experience, facilities, and HR teams scope a breakroom snack-and-beverage program rather than accept a generic operator bundle. It covers the employee survey, four package archetypes, planogram frameworks, ESG sourcing requirements, and the QBR template that keeps the program calibrated each quarter.

1. Pre-launch employee survey template

Run before scoping the package. 10-question survey takes 3-5 minutes; distribute via email + Slack with a 2-week window. Target ≥ 30% response rate.

Q1 — Frequency
How often do you use the breakroom snack/beverage area? (Daily / 3-5×/week / 1-2×/week / Rarely)
Q2 — Preference
Which categories matter most? (Coffee + tea / Cold beverages / Healthy snacks / Indulgent snacks / Fresh food / Plant-based)
Q3 — Dietary
Do you follow a specific diet? (Vegan / Vegetarian / Gluten-free / Kosher / Halal / Low-sugar / Low-sodium / Allergen-aware / None)
Q4 — Time of day
When do you typically use the breakroom? (Morning 7-10 / Mid-morning 10-12 / Lunch 12-2 / Afternoon 2-5 / Late 5+)
Q5 — Pricing
What's a fair price for a snack? ($0.50 / $1.00 / $1.50 / $2.00+) — for beverage? Same scale.
Q6 — Subsidy
Would you value a partially-subsidized program? (Yes — would use more / Same use / No preference)
Q7 — Sustainability
How important is recyclable/compostable packaging? (Very / Somewhat / Not important)
Q8 — Hot drinks
What hot drinks do you order most? (Drip coffee / Espresso-based / Tea / Hot chocolate / None)
Q9 — Brand preferences
Open text — which brands or SKUs you'd want available?
Q10 — Pain points
Open text — what's missing or frustrating today?

4. ESG sourcing checklist

Apply to every SKU bid + every supplier in the chain.

  • Coffee: 100% Fair Trade or Rainforest Alliance; ≥ 50% USDA Organic
  • Chocolate: ≥ 50% Fair Trade or Rainforest Alliance
  • Aluminum cans: ≥ 70% recycled content
  • PET bottles: ≥ 50% rPET
  • Snack films: mono-material PE preferred; How2Recycle Store Drop-Off labeling
  • Compostable cups: BPI-certified; PFAS-free
  • Equipment: 100% ENERGY STAR + 100% R-290 / R-600a refrigerant
  • Telemetry: cellular standard; emission factor per vend supplied annually
  • Three-bin diversion adjacent to every machine
  • Local + minority + women-owned supplier preference (USHCC, NMSDC, WBENC) per host policy

6. Subsidy + pricing model

Free-vend (host covers 100%)
Standard for hospitality + high-retention tech employers. Cost: $25-65/employee/month depending on catalog depth + utilization.
Subsidized partial (host covers 50-100% of base SKUs)
Employees pay nominal $0.25-$0.75 for healthier; full retail for indulgent. Cost: $10-25/employee/month.
Pay-per-cup / per-snack (employees pay full)
Operator-funded; host pays equipment + service only. Lower employee participation (40-60% activation vs. 80-95% free-vend).
Hybrid wellness subsidy
Free-vend on Fit Pick Select + plant-based; conventional at $1-2. Steers behavior without hard restriction.
Wellness-app integration
Steps-to-snack credit via Virgin Pulse / Wellable / Limeade — 10-20% added engagement at minimal incremental cost.

7. Equipment + service specification

Refrigerated equipment
ENERGY STAR-certified, R-290 refrigerant, glass-front + temperature telemetry (33-40°F).
Coffee equipment
Bean-to-cup superautomatic (Franke A300/A600, Schaerer Coffee Club/Soul, WMF 1100 S). 1 brewer per 50-75 employees.
Hot water + tea
Bunn dispenser or instant hot tap (Insinkerator HC2200). Filtered water mandatory.
Telemetry
Cellular, sub-15-minute heartbeat. Cantaloupe / Nayax / 365 / Vagabond.
Service SLA tiers
Tier 1 (offline + payment + temperature) <24 hr; Tier 2 (stockout + planogram) <48 hr; Tier 3 (cosmetic) <14 days.
Restock cadence
Telemetry-driven 1-3×/week per machine; daily for hospitality archetype.

8. Quarterly business review (QBR) template

Operator brings this to every QBR. Workplace-experience + facilities + HR sign-off.

  • Uptime per machine per month (telemetry-verified ≥ 98% target)
  • Per-SKU velocity + top-10 + bottom-10 for the quarter
  • Healthier-share-of-vends trend (target archetype % ± 30%)
  • Plant-based-share-of-vends trend
  • Stockout rate + top-3 stockout SKUs + resolution time
  • Service ticket SLA adherence
  • Cost-per-employee actuals vs. budget; commodity pass-through reconciliation
  • NPS / smile-rating trend (target ≥ 40 year-1, ≥ 50 year-3)
  • Sustainability metrics: kWh, refrigerant, route miles, packaging mix, diversion rate
  • Quarterly SKU refresh: 8-12 new SKUs in / bottom-velocity SKUs out
  • Action items + owner + due date for next quarter

9. Operator selection checklist

  • Catalog flexibility — line-item pricing, not single bundle
  • ENERGY STAR + R-290 equipment standard
  • Telemetry on every machine
  • ESG sourcing alignment per checklist in section 4
  • Wellness-app integration capability
  • References from 3+ comparable-headcount hosts with ≥ 24 months tenure
  • Insurance: $2M GL, $1M product, $1M cyber
  • Termination for convenience with 60-90 day notice
